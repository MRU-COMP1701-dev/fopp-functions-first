    What is the type of a?

x = b.split(',')
z = "".join(x)
y = z.split()
a = "".join(y)

  • integer
  • Not quite, look again at what types are present and what the result of .split() is.
  • float
  • Not quite, look again at what types are present and what the result of .split() is.
  • list
  • Not quite, think about what .split() and .join() return.
